People

Dr. Asadulghani

PRESIDENT - FABA
FABA CHAPTER - BANGLADESH

Dr. Asadulghan i has received his Master of Science (MSc) in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology and joined Molecular Genetics Laboratory of icddr,b on September 1995. He received Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in the field of Molecular Genetics in 2001.

Prof. P. Reddanna

EXECUTIVE PRESIDENT - FABA

Prof P. Reddanna, Senior Professor (Retd.) in the School of Life Sciences, University of Hyderabad, works in the area of inflammation and cancer, specifically in the drug discovery programs. Prof. Reddanna published around 200 papers in peer reviewed journals; Guided 40 students for Ph.D. and trained more than 150 students

Mr. Shakthi Nagappan

PVICE PRESIDENT - FABA

Shakthi Nagappan serves as the Director (Life Sciences and Pharma) for the State Government of Telangana and is in-charge of the Hyderabad Pharma City initiative of the Government, which is one of the biggest Greenfield projects of its nature in Asia, being developed at an area of 19000 acres at international standards.

Prof. Vijay Kumar Kutala

EC MEMBER - FABA

Prof. Vijay Kumar Kutala is serving as Additional Professor in Department of `Clinical Pharmacology & Therapeutics. He is also the Editorial Board member, Gene (Elsevier) Journal, Advisor Board Member, Am J Current Genetics, Autoimmune Diseases and Therapeutic Approaches.

Dr. Palakodeti Ratnakar

SECRETARY GENERAL - FABA

Dr.P Ratnakar is the Vice President & Global Practice Head- Life Sciences and Medical Devices at Tech Mahindra which is a leading provider of digital transformation, consulting and business re-engineering services and solutions. He is responsible for the Global Practice, alliancing and partnerships, Analyst relationships and Co-innovation with start-ups and partners.
